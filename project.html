Project Details

(Project completed on January 30, 2023)

Paint a scenario: someone breaks into your house, steals your precious item, and leaves the safe untouched. Fast forward a few weeks or months, and you open your safe only to find your items missing. Personally, I'd have cried all day. But wait, you are a smart person and have a CCTV camera at home. However you don't know exactly when the theft happened, so you end up spending countless hours scrolling through a month's worth of footage. It's a time-consuming process, and the thief could be long gone.

Now, picture a game-changing concept—a camera with a life. Envision a camera that can cleverly distinguish between its owner and strangers, a camera that knows it's on duty to protect its owner and can spot a theft in action. Ladies and gentlemen, this is where I introduce Kapture to all of you.


Features of KAPTURE

Let's summarize the main features of KAPTURE real quick!

  • 1
    Instant Alert to Theft

    Alarm blasts at signs of theft, triggering instant SMS alerts. Swift notification, quick action—your security, our priority.

  • 2
    Facial Recognition Technology

    Kapture recognizes faces with around 96% accuracy. Precision security, just as you need it.

  • 3
    Smart Zone Monitoring

    Whether it's the front door or a valuable object, Kapture ensures precise monitoring for the zones that matter most to you.

Future Development of KAPTURE

Bringing Security to Your Fingertips

Imagine checking in on your place from anywhere, receiving instant alerts according to your preferences. Our upcoming plans include adding live streaming capabilities, so that you can view real-time footage on your phone.

Kapture is dedicated to making security effortless and personalized—stay tuned for more features to keep you and your home safe!
