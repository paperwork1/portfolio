Project Details

This website was designed for the Front End Web-Development Competition organized by Kathmandu Model College, 2022.
To navigate the website, please click the image above or simply click here.

Theme of the competition? Here it is:

You are a traveler in Kathmandu. However, you’ve got no idea how vast cuisine Kathmandu offers because of the lack of all the information. But after researching and asking locals, you get an overview of all the delicious food items you may have in Kathmandu. You want other visitors to get informed about them effortlessly by simply clicking on a site. And the cherry on top = you know how to make websites!

“Why not create a website where one can find all the information about the cuisines in Kathmandu, along with the best place/restaurant to have them?” you think. So, yes! With all the determination, you start making a website that includes the details.

  • (Spoilers Alert) From Prototype to Actual Website! We showed our design to Lumbini Khaja Ghar, a nearby restaurant, and they absolutely loved our website! They have asked us to build one for them as well. We will soon implement the backend and make it available to them.
