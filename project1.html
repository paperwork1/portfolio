Argo-Forcaster Management System (AFMAS)

AFMAS, currently in prototype, is designed to be the go-to companion for sustainable crop management. Packed with features like disease scanning, expert advice, and a robust alert system, this app is your farm's best friend. It not only identifies potential diseases with a simple scan but also provides expert advice for proactive crop care. The disease alert system ensures you're ahead of any threats, securing the sustainability of your precious crops.
